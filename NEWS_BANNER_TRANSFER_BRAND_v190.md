# Predict2U v190 — News Banner & Transfer Brand

## Included

- A premium responsive hero banner on `news.html`.
- A custom Predict2U Transfers image, optimized as WebP.
- A dedicated Transfer Desk action that activates the Transfers filter.
- A Latest Stories action that scrolls directly to the live feed.
- Source, worldwide coverage and Community-discussion trust cues.
- Custom transfer artwork used when a transfer article has no publisher image.
- Mobile, tablet, desktop and Z Fold layouts.
- Service-worker cache bumped to `predict2u-v190`.
