Predict2U v212 deployment

1. Upload the contents of Predict2U_Device_UI_Repair_v212 to the repository root.
2. Replace matching files.
3. Commit and deploy.
4. Open the app online once so service worker v212 activates.
5. Completely close and reopen the installed PWA.

The My Slip round button is always visible. Drag it to a comfortable screen position; the position is saved on that device. Tap it normally to open the slip drawer.
