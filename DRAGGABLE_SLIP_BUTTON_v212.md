# Predict2U v212 — Draggable Slip Button

- The round My Slip button is now always available, including when the slip is empty.
- It can be dragged anywhere within the usable phone, tablet, or foldable viewport.
- The button is clamped above the bottom app navigation and inside the screen edges.
- Its position is saved locally and restored across page changes, reloads, rotation, and Fold display changes.
- A drag no longer triggers the slip drawer by mistake; a normal tap still opens it.
- The drawer and button use app-safe stacking levels so neither disappears behind the mobile navigation.

- The bottom app navigation is explicitly fixed through 1180px so the draggable button remains clear on tablets and unfolded Z Fold screens.
