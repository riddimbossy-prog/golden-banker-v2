# Predict2U Mobile Release Checklist

This checklist is mandatory for every release after v217.

## Required widths
- 320px narrow phone
- 344px Z Fold cover
- 360px Android phone
- 393px modern phone
- 768px tablet portrait
- 884px unfolded Z Fold / landscape foldable
- 1180px tablet landscape / small laptop
- 1280px and 1440px desktop

## Required pages
- Board with empty, active and settled matches
- Today’s Acca with priced and unpriced selections
- My Slip empty, populated and open
- News with all action buttons
- Proof
- Scorecards
- Community
- Account/Profile
- Engines and League DNA

## Required checks
- No horizontal page overflow.
- Header and bottom navigation do not cover content.
- Floating Slip button stays visible and clear of navigation.
- Buttons remain at least 44px high on touch devices.
- Text does not collide, clip or wrap into unreadable columns.
- External icon/font failure does not create blank controls.
- Loading, empty, offline and error states remain readable.
- Rotation and fold/unfold changes reposition fixed controls safely.
