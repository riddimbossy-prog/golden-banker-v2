# Predict2U v262 — GG Machine, Mismatch Engine and Team Lists

## New public engines
1. **GG Machine v1.0**
   - Home and away split scoring rate: at least 70%
   - Home and away split conceding rate: at least 70%
   - Clean-sheet rate: below 20% for both sides
   - Split PPG: at least 1.50 for both sides
   - BTTS Yes odds: 1.70 or shorter
   - Under 3.5 odds: 1.57 or bigger
   - Minimum eight split matches per side
   - No downgrade market; failed rule means No Bet

2. **Mismatch Engine v2.0**
   - Separate from the older Mismatch specialist for backward-compatible tracking
   - Requires complete opposite statistics with no partial credit
   - Covers result, double chance, team goals, totals, BTTS No and win-either-half routes
   - Strongest Best-v-Worst route requires Best PPG 2.20+, 2.00+ scoring average, top four, unbeaten profile and odds 1.55 or shorter
   - Opposing Worst Team requires PPG 0.80 or lower, scoring 0.80 or lower, bottom four, winless profile and odds 4.50 or bigger
